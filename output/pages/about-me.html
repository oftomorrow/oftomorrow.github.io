About me

Hello everyone

My name is Dinara and I'm a developer and a mathematician that can't imagine life without learning and solving problems. I work as a freelancer since 2013. I use upwork.com for my freelancing. Currently I am working as a AI/ML Developer for DeepSine company.

Brief history

I started writing code since I was 11 years old. I didn't have an internet access yet, therefore I went to a special class after school. I was studying TurboPascal there. I really loved it and then I decided to become a developer. Also I loved math all the time I attended the school. I was dreaming to become mathematician since my preliminary year.

I graduated from the Ufa State Aviation Technical University in 2011. I studied mathematics and computer science. At that time we couldn't choose a degree in the University at all. All of us studied for 5 years and got certificate of Specialist's degree.

This blog was created to unveil my potential in Data Science and developing and to discuss topics and problems with people holding the same view.
